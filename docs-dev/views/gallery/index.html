Hot Dawa Tea

Dawa means medicine in Swahili and so it is fitting that a hot dawa tea is commonly used to soothe a sore throat and ease a cold or flu.
Aside from its medicinal properities, dawa tea is great for rainy day, a chilly night, or any time you need a strong kick of ginger!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 ginger ( 1 tablespoon of grated ginger )
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • Optional:
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 5 clove seeds
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 lemons

Directions

  1. Add water and ginger, bring to a boil.
  2. Add turmeric, cinnamon stick and clove seeds, let simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Strain solids from the water and pour into a glass jar.
  4. Add the honey, freshly squeezed lemon, and stir
  5. Enjoy!
